Icon samples

Lucide outline set. Every tile uses an icon from Lucide (ISC license), inlined as SVG: viewBox="0 0 24 24", stroke-width="1", stroke="#2c2c2c", round caps/joins. Display size 48px. Sample assets under public/icons/samples/ are vector-only (no PNG/JPEG). Regenerate: node scripts/build-icons-preview.mjs.
